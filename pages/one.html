What is PACE?

PACE is NASA's Plankton, Aerosol, Cloud, ocean Ecosystem mission

PACE mission is a NASA satellite launched in the 2020s to observe Earth's oceans, atmosphere, and ecosystems. PACE helps track ocean color, phytoplankton blooms, and air quality, contributing to a better understanding of our planet. Its data benefits society by supporting water quality, fisheries, and food security.
